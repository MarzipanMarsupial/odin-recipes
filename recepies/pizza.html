Bakery Style Pizza

Freshly baked Bakery Style Pizza

10/10 Goodieness!

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1+1/4 cups warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1+1/2 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil, divided

Sauce:

  • 1(28 ounce) can plain crushed tomatoes
  • 1(14 ounce)can (generic)pizza sauce
