Protein Powder Overnight Oats with Blueberries

Refreshing jar of overnight oats

Pure gold

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup almond milk

  • ½ cup oats

  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds

  • 2 tablespoons agave nectar

  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder

  • 1 cup frozen blueberries

Steps:

  1. Mix almond milk, oats, chia seeds, agave, and protein powder in a bowl until well combined. Divide mixture between 2 Mason jars. Top each jar with 1/2 cup blueberries. Refrigerate for 8 hours, or overnight.
